Mashed Potatoes :

Description :

This is my go to recipe for quick and delicous mashed potatoes!

Ingredients :

  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Potatoes
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps :

  1. Boil some potatoes until thoroughly cooked.
  2. Drain them.
  3. Mash them.
  4. Add butter to the potatoes.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add cream until you get the consitency you desire.
  7. Mix them and plate them up.
  8. Enjoy your mashed potatoes!
