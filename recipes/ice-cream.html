Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

Description :

This is my go to recipe for a quick and delicous ice cream!

Ingredients :

  • Whipping Cream
  • Oreos
  • Condensed Milk

Steps :

  1. Whip a cup of whipping cream until they reach a soft peaks stage.
  2. Crush up as much Oreos as you would like.
  3. Add half of a cup condensed milk to the whipping cream.
  4. Add in the crushed Oreos.
  5. Fold all the ingredients together.
  6. Freeze until solid and you've got your ice cream!
