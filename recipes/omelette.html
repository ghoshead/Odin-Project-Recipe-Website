Cookies and Cream Ice Cream :

Description :

This is my go to recipe for a quick and delicous ice cream!

Ingredients :

  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Onions

Steps :

  1. Whip as much eggs as youd like.
  2. Add salt and pepper to the eggs according to taste.
  3. Dice up onions and add them to the mixture.
  4. Cook until almost done and plate the omelette.
  5. Enjoy your omelette with some toast and a glass of milk or juice!
